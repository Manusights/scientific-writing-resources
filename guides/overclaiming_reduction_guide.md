# Overclaiming Reduction Guide

Overclaiming is one of the most common reasons reviewers lose trust.

## Where overclaiming shows up
- title and abstract
- discussion conclusions
- mechanistic language beyond evidence

## Reduction method
For each strong claim, add:
- direct supporting evidence
- uncertainty or boundary statement
- language that matches design strength

## Rewrite pattern
Replace broad impact statements with specific measured findings.

## Team check
Ask one reviewer to mark any sentence they see as stronger than the underlying data.
Revise all flagged lines before submission.
