# Case Study: Abstract and Discussion Revision

## Scenario
Draft had vague language and broad claims not tied to numbers.

## Revision approach
- replaced adjectives with measured outcomes
- inserted confidence intervals for primary endpoint
- added clear limitation statements in discussion

## Result
- abstract became specific and defensible
- discussion became aligned with evidence boundaries
