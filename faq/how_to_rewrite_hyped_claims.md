# How to Rewrite Hyped Claims

Hyped: "This method dramatically changes the field."

Better: "This method improved endpoint X by 9.4% in the tested cohort."


## Rewrite drill
Take one paragraph, remove adjectives, add exact numbers, and keep one claim per sentence.
