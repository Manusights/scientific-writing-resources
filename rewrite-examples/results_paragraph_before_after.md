# Results Paragraph: Before and After

## Before
"The intervention showed strong improvement and clear clinical promise across groups."

## After
"At day 90, response improved by 10.7% (95% CI 3.2 to 17.5) versus control in the prespecified primary cohort."

## Why this is better
- exact endpoint stated
- effect size and uncertainty provided
- claim scope bounded to prespecified cohort
